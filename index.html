About Me

Hi! I'm an Information Science major at the University of Maryland with a minor in Data Science. I've always been curious about finding ways to turn complex information into something that people can better understand, which is what drew me to a field that brings together data, systems, and the people who interact with them.

  • Resume


What I’ve Explored

Over the past few years, I've explored that through projects in analytics, machine learning, databases, web applications, and user research. Working on problems in healthcare, business, and consumer spaces has taught me that good solutions come from understanding both the information itself and the needs of the stakeholders involved.

What I Enjoy Doing

I enjoy building structure from messy information, finding patterns that matter, and turning all of that into something tangible—whether it's a dashboard, application, workflow, or a better user experience. I like work that requires both analytical thinking and creativity, and I appreciate opportunities to collaborate with others and learn from different perspectives.

Looking Ahead

As I begin my career, I hope to continue building technology that helps people make sense of information and make better decisions. In the long term, I want to contribute to products and systems that are thoughtful, practical, and genuinely useful to the people they serve.
